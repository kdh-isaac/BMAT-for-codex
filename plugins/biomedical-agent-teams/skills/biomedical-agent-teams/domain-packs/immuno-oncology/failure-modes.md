# Immuno-Oncology Failure Modes

- Do not convert bulk TME association into tumor-intrinsic or CAR-T-intrinsic mechanism without cell-type-resolved evidence.
- Do not treat checkpoint, cytotoxicity, exhaustion, myeloid suppression, or interferon signatures as causal without perturbation or longitudinal support.
- For CAR-T or adoptive-cell therapy claims, separate product-level phenotype, tumor-context association, and patient outcome association.
- For scRNA-seq, require donor/sample-aware statistics before cross-patient prognostic or therapeutic-response claims.
- For deconvolution, report the proxy method and avoid naming exact cellular mechanisms when the bulk proxy cannot distinguish them.
- For survival/prognosis, define event/censor variables, cohort source/version, grouping rule, covariates, event counts, and multiple-testing control.
