# Cell-Therapy Failure Modes

- Bulk-to-CAR-T-intrinsic overclaim: tumor bulk or product-level association is written as cell-intrinsic mechanism.
- Donor/sample leakage: cell-level tests are treated as biological replicate support.
- Product-context drift: axi-cel, tisa-cel, liso-cel, trial cohort, or manufacturing context is collapsed.
- Exhaustion/activation ambiguity: activation markers are interpreted as dysfunction without timepoint and phenotype controls.
- Safety translation overreach: in vitro or mouse efficacy is written as clinical actionability.
